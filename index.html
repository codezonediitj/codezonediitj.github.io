So what actually CodeZoned is?

For us computer science is less of a subject and more of a girlfriend or boyfriend, depends ;-) We learn AI, ML, DL, CP, and obviously the underlying mathematics.
Uh! we were too busy with CS that we didn't have the time to edit the pic below. Make a PR if you have a better one.

So let's give you a complete overview. We basically are a group of people on gitter and whatsapp and there we share, a lot of tech doubts, some useful resources for learning. We firmly believe in open sourcing stuff. Till now we have a huge collection of resources from a lot of contributors, licensed under the MIT license.

As you can see in the navbar, we have a blog, known as TechInfo, where you can write your own thoughts about tech. Check it out and get some idea. To add your blog entry, just make a PR to the website's repo. It will be up as soon as it is merged.

We also promote online tech events for free. Just scroll down a little bit and send us a message. :-) We don't have the money so only 50 requests per month will be accepted. :-( Learn, share and explore. See you soon!!
